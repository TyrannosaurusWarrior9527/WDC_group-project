Welcome to TravelDreams

Easiest way to access to popular tourist attractions around the world

TravelDreams is a web application which use to encourage users' passion for travel, focusing on show popular tourist attractions around the world and introduce the stories behind them. Users can look around beautiful photos and very detailed travel guides and mark their favorite destinations, rate attractions, and write reasons for wanting to visit or travel notes. Through rating and tagging features users can easily record and plan their travel and experience.
Besides, TravelDreams integrates third-party APIs to make sure information is automatically updated and there is large amount of content accessible.
